Easy Hummus Recipe

Making hummus in an Instapot is incredibly easy. Simply dump a pound of garbanzo beans in the Instapot, add water, then add pumpkin, tomato sauce, and spices. Make sure to not stir the pot and allow it to cook as is. Once the "Beans" setting on the Instapot is done, allow the mixture to cool, then put a cup or so into a food processor and blend into easy Hummus!
